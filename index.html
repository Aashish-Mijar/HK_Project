About Y-Center Nepal

Building digital competence in small towns of Nepal

Y-Center Nepal was founded with the vision of establishing digital skills development centers in small towns across Nepal, starting with Waling, Syangja. Our mission is to accelerate IT workforce creation and facilitate IT entrepreneurship by leveraging the global network of Nepali IT professionals.

We believe in the "Made in Nepal for Nepal" spirit and are committed to creating opportunities for youth in small towns to participate in the digital economy.
