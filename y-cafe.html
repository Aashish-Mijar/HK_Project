More Than Just a Learning Space

A community hub for connection, inspiration, and local products

Y-Center Nepal is more than just a digital skills training center. We've created a vibrant ecosystem that includes Y-Cafe, a Book Shop, and a Made in Nepal Products Shop. These spaces are designed to foster community, provide resources, and showcase local talent and products.

Whether you're looking for a quiet place to work, quality books on technology and entrepreneurship, or unique locally-made products, our Y-Cafe & Shops have something for everyone.

Y-Cafe

A cozy space to work, connect, and recharge

Y-Cafe is a comfortable and inspiring space where students, entrepreneurs, and community members can work, study, or simply relax. With high-speed internet, comfortable seating, and a welcoming atmosphere, it's the perfect place to boost your productivity or have meaningful conversations.

What We Offer:

High-Speed Internet

Stay connected with our reliable high-speed internet, perfect for remote work or online learning.

Quality Beverages

Enjoy a selection of locally-sourced coffee, tea, and other refreshing beverages.

Light Snacks

Fuel your work sessions with our range of healthy snacks and light meals.

Community Events

Join our regular community events, from book clubs to networking sessions.

Workspaces

Choose from individual desks, collaborative tables, or comfortable lounge areas.

Power Outlets

Plenty of power outlets ensure your devices stay charged throughout the day.

Made in Nepal Products

Supporting local artisans and entrepreneurs

Our Made in Nepal Products Shop showcases and sells high-quality products created by local artisans and entrepreneurs. We believe in supporting local talent and providing a platform for Nepali creators to reach a wider audience. Each product in our shop has a story and contributes to the local economy.

Product Categories:

Handcrafted Apparel

Clothing and accessories made with traditional Nepali techniques and modern designs.

Home Decor

Natural Products

Art & Crafts
