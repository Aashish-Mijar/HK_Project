Our Vision

To establish one 'Y Centre' for a population of around 1 lac in small towns of Nepal, creating a vibrant ecosystem for digital competence acceleration and IT entrepreneurship.

Our Mission

Accelerating IT workforce creation and facilitating IT entrepreneurship in small towns of Nepal by leveraging the global network of Nepali IT professionals.

Our Story

Building digital competence in small towns of Nepal



Y-Center Nepal was founded in 2024 by three visionary Nepali professionals - Dinkar Nepal, Sunil Karki, and Bibek Koirala - who shared a common dream of bringing digital opportunities to small towns across Nepal.

Starting in Waling, Syangja, our founders recognized that while Nepal's major cities were experiencing digital growth, small towns were being left behind. They envisioned creating spaces where youth in these areas could gain digital skills, connect with global opportunities, and build sustainable IT careers without leaving their hometowns.

The name "Y-Center" represents the convergence of Youth, Yield, and You - symbolizing our commitment to empowering young people, yielding positive outcomes, and putting you at the center of everything we do.

What began as a small training center has grown into a comprehensive ecosystem that includes digital skills training, entrepreneurship support, a co-working space, a caf√©, and a bookshop - all designed to nurture the next generation of IT professionals and entrepreneurs in Nepal's small towns.

Today, Y-Center Nepal continues to expand its impact, with plans to establish centers in more small towns across Nepal, bringing digital opportunities to every corner of the country.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Community First

We prioritize the needs of our local communities, striving to understand their unique challenges and create meaningful solutions. By fostering collaboration and innovation, we aim to address real-world issues that matter most to the people around us. Our commitment is to empower communities with sustainable, impactful initiatives that drive positive change.

Innovation

We embrace creative thinking and cutting-edge technologies to tackle challenges, unlock potential, and drive meaningful change.

Inclusivity

We ensuring that digital opportunities are accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or prior experience, is at the heart of our mission.

Global Perspective

While connecting local talent to international opportunities, we remain committed to our "Made in Nepal for Nepal" spirit, fostering homegrown innovation.

Mentorship

We believe in the transformative power of guidance and support from experienced professionals to nurture new talent and expand possibilities.

Sustainable Growth

Our focus is on building skills and businesses that create long-term value, empowering individuals and communities for lasting success.
