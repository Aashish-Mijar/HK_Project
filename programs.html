Comprehensive programs designed for the needs of small-town Nepal

At Y-Center Nepal, we offer a range of programs designed to equip youth with the skills, knowledge, and support they need to thrive in the digital economy. Whether you're looking to start a career in IT, launch your own tech startup, or simply enhance your digital literacy, we have a program for you. All our programs are taught by experienced professionals and include hands-on projects to ensure you gain practical experience.

IT Entrepreneurship

Launch and grow your tech startup

Our IT Entrepreneurship program is designed to help aspiring entrepreneurs turn their ideas into viable businesses. We provide guidance, resources, and support throughout the startup journey, from ideation to launch and growth.

Program Components:

Idea Validation

Learn how to validate your business idea through market research, customer interviews, and competitive analysis.

Business Planning

Develop a comprehensive business plan, including market strategy, financial projections, and growth plans.

MVP Development

Build a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) to test your concept with real users and gather feedback.

Funding Strategies

Learn about different funding options and how to pitch your startup to potential investors.

Marketing & Growth

Develop marketing strategies to acquire and retain customers and scale your business.

Networking

Connect with other entrepreneurs, mentors, and potential investors through our network.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs

What are the eligibility requirements for your programs?

Most of our programs are open to anyone aged 16 and above with basic computer literacy. Specific courses may have additional prerequisites, which are mentioned in the course details.

Do I need to have prior coding experience to join your digital skills courses?

For beginner courses like Web Development Fundamentals, no prior coding experience is required. For advanced courses, basic programming knowledge may be necessary. Each course listing specifies the prerequisites.

Are scholarships available for your programs?

Yes, we offer scholarships for deserving candidates who demonstrate financial need and strong motivation. You can apply for scholarships through our website or by visiting our center in Waling.

How can I become a mentor in your mentorship program?

If you're an experienced professional in the tech industry and would like to give back to the community, you can apply to become a mentor through our website. We'll review your application and schedule an interview.

Do you provide job placement assistance after completing your courses?

Yes, we have a career support program that helps graduates find job opportunities, prepare for interviews, and build their professional network. We also connect students with internship opportunities at partner companies.

Can I attend your programs remotely?

Some of our courses and workshops are available in hybrid format, allowing remote participation. However, we encourage in-person attendance when possible to benefit from hands-on guidance and community interaction.
