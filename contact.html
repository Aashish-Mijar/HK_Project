Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting and visiting us

How can I enroll in one of your courses?

You can enroll in our courses by visiting our center in Waling, calling us at +977 9821328051, or filling out the enrollment form on our website. Our team will guide you through the enrollment process and answer any questions you may have.

Do you offer virtual consultations for those who can't visit in person?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations via video call for those who cannot visit our center in person. You can schedule a virtual consultation by contacting us through email or phone.

How can I become a mentor or volunteer at Y-Center Nepal?

We welcome mentors and volunteers who are passionate about our mission. Please fill out the contact form on this page, selecting "Mentorship Program" or "Volunteering" as your inquiry type, and our team will get in touch with you to discuss opportunities.

Can I visit Y-Center Nepal without an appointment?

Yes, you're welcome to visit Y-Center Nepal during our opening hours without an appointment. However, if you'd like to speak with a specific team member or learn about a particular program, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure the right person is available to assist you.

How can organizations partner with Y-Center Nepal?

We're always open to partnerships that align with our mission. Organizations interested in partnering with Y-Center Nepal can contact us through the form on this page, selecting "Partnership Opportunities" as the inquiry type, or email us directly at partnerships@ycenternepal.com.

Is Y-Center Nepal accessible by public transportation?

Yes, Y-Center Nepal is accessible by public transportation. We're located near the main bus stop in Waling, and local taxis are familiar with our location. If you need specific directions, please contact us, and we'll be happy to help.
